About Me

Get to know a little about me.

Hello there, my name is Devkomo - Kow Ahenasi Amonoo, a coder and designer from Accra-Ghana. I am passionate about graphic designing and coding for the web. My skills and talents are available for the resolution of creative problems. Get in touch, lets start something awesome together!

Services

Get to know the services I am adept at.

 

General Design

Research, planning and designing of design schemes and projects that are solely meant for screen. This is my first foray into the screen only side of design.

 

Print Design

For over a decade, this has being my stock-in-trade. This covers almost every aspect of design for printing from corporate mementos and stationery to large format print schemes.

 

Web Development

Website coding and design are the next step for my tinkering fingers. I am as passionate about this as I am excited by what can be achieved with it.
